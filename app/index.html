Here’s a little bit about ourselves.

So you’re moving to a new country to study abroad? Congratulations! You’ve just made an important and life-changing first step. But moving abroad can be a daunting task: you need to make new friends, find a place to live and of course find out where the best bars, clubs, and cinema’s are. But no worries, we are here to help you!

At Student Buddy, we are specialised in providing the best introduction to your new student life. Your will pair up with local students, called ‘buddies’, who will guide you through the city. How? With fun activities all across town, a handbook to help you get settled, an entertainment tour, and lots more.

Our buddy system will make sure that you don’t feel left out when you arrive in a new country, full of questions and feeling lost in translation. After all, we’ve been there before and know what we need to do to make you feel welcome!

Our Features

Local Guides

We pair everyone with an excited local student who can’t wait to show you around. These guys and girls know everything there is to know about the city, so let’s meet up!

Handbook

Everyone receives a handbook full of tips and tricks, such as the best places to go out, where to watch a movie, or shop. You will find everything you need in here!

App

In our Student Buddy app you can get in touch with your buddy, meet other students from abroad and get regular updates on what’s happening in town.

Goodie Bag

Of course you can’t be welcomed empty handed, so we provide you with a great goodie bag, a survival kit. It includes our handbook, and some more mystery presents essential for your introduction time!
